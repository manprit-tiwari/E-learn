Introduction to BBA-CA

What is BBA(CA)?
BBA(CA) is a three years undergraduate course under Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, Maharashtra. And In this course students are tought all about computer application which are design for end users so they can perform the they intend. This program lasts for three years and equips students with computer as well as Management skills.
Eligibility for BBA(CA)?
An applicant who has passed HSC examination of an equivalent examination of any board or university with 45% Marks in Aggregate with english as one of the Passing subject is eligible for addmission to First Year BBA-CA degree course every eligible candidate has to pass comman entrance test to be conducted by the respective institute/college.
Difference between BBA-CA from BCA and BBA
BBA-CA is not the same as BCA or BBA only it sounds similar but it is more advanced course which has the syllabus which the industries will be using in near future or in present so that will be ready for upcomming future.
